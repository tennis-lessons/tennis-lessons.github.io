About Us

We are a group of passionate tennis coaches in Singapore. We are looking for students of the sports who seeks to improve fundamentally on their skills sets. We welcome players of all ages and proficiency and provides all group and individual tennis classes.

We organized groups tennis lessons focusing in the West area and student also the options of having private tennis lesson conducted at their own preferred timing and venue.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and certified tennis coaches

  • Flexible Timing and location of your preferred choice

  • Lessons are structured in fun and conductive ways for students to progress

  • All equipment is provided, no fret if you do not own a tennis racket! Just come along in your sports attire!

  • Effective system to conduct all tennis classes for kids and adults

  • Small groups to ensure maximum efficiency in the lessons

    • More quality practice time for the students
    • Coaches can allocate more attention on weaker students
    • Motivating and competitive environment
    • Coaches can monitor student progress more closely and provide frequent feedbacks
